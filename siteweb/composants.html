Ultrason HC-SR04 Sensor


An ultrasonic sensor emits short, high-frequency sound pulses at regular intervals. These pulses propagate in the air at the speed of sound. When they meet an object, they are reflected and back in form echo.

• Supply voltage: 5V DC.
• Supply current: 15mA.
• Action cone: ≈15°.
• Range of detection: entre 3cm et 3m.
• Dimension: 45*20*15mm.
• Transmitter input signal: Impulse in the high state of 10 µs.




Infrared detector RKI-3141


Since the sensor module is adaptable to ambient light and has a pair of infrared emitter and receiver tubes, the emitting tubes emit a certain infrared frequency when the direction of an obstacle is detected (reflecting surface).

• Angle of detection: 35°.
• The effective distance range of 2 ~ 30 cm.
• Operating voltage: 3.3 V-5 V.
• Panel size: 3.2 CM * 1.4 CM.




Buzzer


Un buzzer is an electromechanical or electronic element that produces a sound when a voltage is applied to it. Some require a DC voltage, others require an AC voltage.

• Dimension: 3.3 x 1.3cm.
• Its frequency between 500 & 5kHz.
• Operating Voltage 3V à 30V.
• It works on a pin PWM.
