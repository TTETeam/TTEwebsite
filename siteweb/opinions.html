The TTE is a phenomenal project that will drastically benefit the community. It gives an alternative to the traditional white canes by adding to it some distinct features that enhances both, the speed and confidence of the visually impaired people. By creating a beep sound whenever the persons safety is about to be altered, it ensures that the visually impaired people are always safe while navigating and never walk into something. Additionally, one of the major benefits is that it does not require any training to be used, which makes it an easy tool that can be used by everyone. Finally, TTE project is an efficient way to improve the lifestyle of visually impaired people.
